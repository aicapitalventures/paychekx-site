C. How triage works

The board sorts based on damage risk and time sensitivity.

Tier 2, should payMeaningful consequences, but survivable if delayed short term.
Tier 3, can delayLow immediate damage or negotiable without collapse.
Tier 4, cut or freezeNon essential, pause until margin stabilizes.
