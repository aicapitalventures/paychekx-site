# PRIVACY POLICY  
**PAYCHEKX**

---

## 1. Overview

PAYCHEKX is designed as a **privacy-first product**.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

All tools run locally in your browser.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

PAYCHEKX does not collect:

- Names  
- Email addresses  
- Account information  
- Financial data  
- IP addresses  
- Device identifiers  
- Usage analytics  
- Cookies  
- Tracking pixels  

We do not use:

- Google Analytics  
- Facebook Pixel  
- Session recording  
- Behavioral tracking  

---

## 3. How the Product Works

All PAYCHEKX tools:

- Run entirely in your browser  
- Do not send data to any server  
- Do not save data remotely  
- Do not share data with third parties  

Any information you enter remains on your device.

---

## 4. Local Storage

Some features may use your browser’s local storage to:

- Remember tool selection (Single Tool tier)  
- Maintain session continuity  

This data:

- Never leaves your device  
- Is not accessible to us  
- Can be cleared by clearing your browser storage  

---

## 5. Payments

Payments are processed by **Stripe**.

We do not receive or store:

- Card numbers  
- Bank information  
- Billing details  

Please refer to Stripe’s Privacy Policy for payment data handling.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

PAYCHEKX uses:

- Stripe (payments only)  
- Static hosting providers (page delivery only)

We do not embed third-party tracking or advertising services.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

PAYCHEKX is not directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
