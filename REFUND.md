PAYCHEKX Refund Policy

PAYCHEKX offers a limited refund policy to protect users from technical failure while preventing abuse.

Refund Window

Refunds may be requested within:

7 calendar days of purchase

Eligible Refund Reasons

Tool does not load or function

Tier access routing failure

Critical bug preventing use

Ineligible Refund Reasons

Buyer’s remorse

Changed financial situation

Failure to read product description

Clearing browser data and losing access

Choosing the wrong tool under Single Tool tier

How to Request a Refund

Send an email to:

support@paychekx.com

Include:

Stripe receipt ID

Date of purchase

Reason for refund

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within:

3–5 business days

Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback instead of contacting support may result in permanent access revocation.
